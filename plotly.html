Introduction

A subset of the NYC Restaurant Inspections dataset is used to create the following graphs. For the flexdashboard click here

Dataset Used

  • Excludes:
    • missing borough (boro) information
    • observations with NAs for score variable.
  • Includes:
    • inspection dates in 2013 to 2017 (5 year data)
    • only observations that received grades A, B, or C.
    Resulting dataset has 188189 observations and 19 variables.

Bar Graph: Counts Across 5 Boroughs

In this dataset, Manhattan has the highest count (74.8k) while Staten Island has the least (6,355).

Manhattan Bar Graph: Grade vs. Score

  • In Manhattan, entities that received a C grade have a median score of 34 with an outlier scoring 83 points.
    • As the grade increases from A to C, the median scores also increases.

Manhattan Scatterplot: Zip code vs. Score by Cuisine (Chinese, Japanese, Korean)

This scatterplot contains observations of Manhattan entities that received a grade (A, B, or C) in 2017. There are 1,894 observations.
